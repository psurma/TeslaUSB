Schedule

The archive timer runs 2 minutes after boot, then every 5 minutes. Syncs only happen when:

  • NAS archiving is enabled in config.yaml
  • Connected to home WiFi ({{ archive.config.home_ssid or '(not set)' }})
  • USB is in present mode (serving to Tesla)
  • TeslaCam drive is mounted

Check timer status via SSH: systemctl status nas_archive.timer
